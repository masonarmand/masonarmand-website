Gupta

Gupta is a game I made in about two weeks for my Experimental Game Jams website. Gupta is written in C and uses the Raylib game library. The theme for the jam was grid, so I decided to make a roguelike dungeon crawler with chess based movement. You can combine different segments of your 'chess piece' to alter your move pattern. There is also modifiers you can buy that allow you to do things such as mining or exploding surrounding blocks.
